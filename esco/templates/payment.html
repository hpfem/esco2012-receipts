Payment Information

Registration fees for ESCO 2010 conference are as follows:

Registration fee By March 15, 2010 After March 15, 2010
Regular participants 350 EUR 500 EUR
Student participants 250 EUR 400 EUR
Accompanying persons 200 EUR 250 EUR

The regular and student fees cover all conference materials, book of abstracts, conference lunches, coffee breaks, welcome reception, farewell party, and excursion. The accompanying person fee covers conference lunches, welcome reception, farewell party, and excursion. In order to qualify for the reduced student rate, a scanned copy of a valid student ID must be attached to the registration form and payment confirmation.
