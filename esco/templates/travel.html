Travel Information

Registration of participants will take place in the lobby of the hotel Angelo Pilsen on Sunday June 27, 2010. Dinner will be provided.

Using a train from Prague

This is a recommended way of transportation. The trains leave from the Prague Main Railway Station (Praha Hlavni Nadrazi) often and the trip takes less than 2 hours. When coming through the Prague Airport, use the Airport Express shuttle service which takes you there directly. When coming from other parts of the city, the Main Railway Station is a stop of the red line "C" of Prague subway. Train tickets can be purchased in the lobby of the railway station, or directly in the train (higher fees apply). After arriving to Pilsen, take a taxi to the Angelo hotel. It might be a good idea to ask about the price in advance.
